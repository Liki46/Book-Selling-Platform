Key to the future is here.

The main Target of the book is to give an outline view of complex topics in physics. This one particular oversimplified version aims to bring confidence in readers of all age groups. By avoiding numerical and any derivations, we tried to make it look simple and to bring out interest in reader. Once, the readers are done reading, there will definitely be a small anxiety to further know about equations and mathematical applications of the given topics. Thus the book becomes successful in creating Confidence and anxiety, required to overcome the belief that physics is a difficult subject.
